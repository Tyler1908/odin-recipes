Chicken Caesar Salad

This classic chicken caesar salad is sure to satiate any individual with the mix of lettuce, chicken, cheese, and seasoned croutons. Ready in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups tightly packed chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 pound cooked boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1/2 cup CRACKER BARREL finely shredded 100% parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup seasoned croutons
  • 1/4 cup KRAFT creamy caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Cover 4 plates with lettuce.
  2. Top with all remaining ingredients except dressing.
  3. Drizzle with dressing just before serving.
